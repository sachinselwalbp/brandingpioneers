Meet Dr. Abhishek Kumar Mishra, Best Orthopaedic Surgeon In Delhi NCR.

Dr. Abhishek Kumar Mishra is a senior orthopaedic and joint replacement surgeon with an experience of over 15 years. He has completed his MBBS from MAMC, Delhi (1997), MS in Orthopaedics from LHMC, Delhi (2003) MRCS from Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons, Glasgow, UK (2005) and M.Ch. in Orthopaedics. Dr. Mishra is internationally trained and specialises in Knee Replacement, Hip Replacement and Minimally Invasive Spine Surgeries. He won a gold medal in his post-graduation and has done several fellowships in different countries like Germany, Austria, Hong-Kong, USA, Singapore etc. Dr. Mishra is also a member of several prestigious Orthopaedic Associations in India and Abroad.
